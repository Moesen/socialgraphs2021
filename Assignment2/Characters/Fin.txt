{{Term/Store|Fin||Series, BotW}} is a character in {{BotW}}.<ref>{{Cite|Fin|N/A|BotW}}</ref>

==Biography==
Fin is a member of the Shield Breakers that camps out near [[Woodland Stable]].<ref>{{Cite|It's cause we're members of the {{Color|BotW Red|shield-surfing}} gang, the Shield Breakers!|Russ|BotW}}</ref> Upon talking to him, he asks Link if he is up for "shield-surfing".<ref>{{Cite|Hey, maaan! What's that? You're dyin' for some {{Color|BotW Red|shield-surfing}} action, that it?|Fin|BotW}}</ref> If Link agrees to try out shield-surfing, Fin points him to his best friend, [[Russ]], under a tree on top of the hill.<ref>{{Cite|My best bud is standin' over there beneath that tree! He can show you the ropes.|Fin|BotW}}</ref>

After reaching the top of the hill, Russ apologizes to Link for Fin pressuring him into shield-surfing, and offers him shields to buy.<ref>{{Cite|Heeey, man. Nice of you to stop by! Sorry about {{Color|BotW Blue|Fin}} strong-armin' ya into comin' up here, heh.|Russ|BotW}}</ref>
