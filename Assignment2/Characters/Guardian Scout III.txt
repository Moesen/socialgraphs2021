==Characteristics==
==={{BotW|-}}===
{{Compendium/Extract|Guardian Scout III}}
The Guardian Scout III is the third variation of the [[Guardian Scout]]. They're found inside "A Moderate Test of Strength" {{Plural|BotW|Ancient Shrine|link}}, which require {{Term|BotW|Link|link}} to defeat them in order to complete the shrine. Unlike the [[Guardian Scout II]], the Guardian Scout III has the ability to dual-wield weapons, which can be any combination of a [[Guardian Sword+]], [[Guardian Shield+]], [[Guardian Shield+]], and [[Ancient Battle Axe+]].<ref>{{Cite|This model is equipped with twin-blade functionality to further test the combat prowess of one undertaking the trials.|Hyrule Compendium|BotW}}</ref>
