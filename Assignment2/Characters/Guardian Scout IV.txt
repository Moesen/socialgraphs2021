{{Term/Store|Guardian Scout IV|Guardian Scouts IV|Series, BotW|plural}} are enemies in {{BotW}}.<ref>{{Cite|'''{{Color|BotW Shrine Quest|Guardian Scout IV}}'''|Hyrule Compendium|BotW}}</ref>

==Characteristics==
{{Compendium/Extract|Guardian Scout IV}}
The Guardian Scout IV is the strongest variation of the [[Guardian Scout]], found within "A Major Test of Strength" {{Plural|BotW|Ancient Shrine|link}}. In order to complete these shrines, {{Term|BotW|Link|link}} must defeat the Guardian Scouts IV inside. Being the most upgraded versions, Guardian Scouts IV are capable of wielding three weapons at once.<ref>{{Cite|This model is very resilient and has been outfitted with triple-blade functionality, allowing it to wield three weapons. This will put any would-be hero to the test for sure.|Hyrule Compendium|BotW}}</ref> They are equipped with any combination of a [[Guardian Sword++]], [[Guardian Shield++]], [[Guardian Spear++]], or [[Ancient Battle Axe++]].
