{{Term/Store|Damia||Series}} is a character in {{BotW}}.{{Name Ref|BotW}}

==Biography==
Damia is a [[Korok]] that lives in the [[Korok Forest]].  After [[Link]] starts "[[The Korok Trials]]" [[Side Quest]], he can meet with Damia by following the eastern path out of the forest.  Link can speak with Damia to attempt "[[The Test of Wood]]" [[Shrine Quest]].
